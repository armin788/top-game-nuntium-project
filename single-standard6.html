Fixed the basic bugs of Battlefield 2042 in the new game update

  • newgame

EA DICE Studios has released a new update to Battlefield 2042 for console and PC gamers to fix some major issues. The Battlefield 2042 update is specifically designed to fix basic bugs and balance the overall gaming experience. One of the most important issues is fixing problems that prevented some users from having a consistent gameplay experience. Dice, for example, says there is no longer a problem with players being revived by their teammates. In addition, the new update focused specifically on vehicles and a number of game weapons to increase the balance of gameplay; From LCAA Hovercraft and MD540 Nightbird to KA-520 Super Hokum and AH-64GX Apache Warchief. In this update, an attempt has been made to make them more compatible with other items. In terms of improving the performance of game weapons, we can mention a slight increase in the PP-29 kick and "greater stability of continuous firing of weapons." The development team also made a brief reference to the content of the third update being built on Battlefield 2042, which is claimed to be the biggest update to the game to date. This update focuses specifically on improving the game's performance, and in various ways, the technical condition of the game will improve the first-person shooter.

Countless Battlefield 2042 problems during release, including severe accidental deflection of bullets, uncoordinated heatboxes, poor technical performance on various PCs, misleading weapons personalization screen, and lack of a correct scoreboard, caused players to react strongly to the game. And post thousands of negative comments on Steam. In the review of Battlefield 2042 by Mohammad Sadegh Tavazeei, Zomji gave a score of 7 out of 10 to the new EA DICE. In a part of the article, we read that "presenting a sense of nostalgia with the portal, the existence of Hazard Zone and its new structure for the Battlefield series" are the distinguishing aspects of this game in the Battlefield series. "Battlefield 2042 is a product that brings new and fun ideas, but the lack of proper use of the potential of these ideas has prevented Battlefield 2042 from being as entertaining as it should be, and perhaps a fun product for users," the reviewer concluded. "He is a newcomer as well as an old player in the series." Battlefield 2042 is currently available for gamers on PlayStation 5, Xbox X Series, Xbox S Series, PlayStation 4, Xbox One and PC.
