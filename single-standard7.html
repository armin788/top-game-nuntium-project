Release of the new material of Counter Strike game with the focus on the Squid Game series

  • newgamevideo

Players can experience all the Squid Game series competitions thanks to a new item in Counter-Strike: Global Offensive. In the latest game news, we learned that Counter-Strike players can experience all the games in the Squid Game series thanks to a completely new medium. While Netflix may one day actually make Squid Game, this material, available in the Steam Workshop, allows gamers to experience all the in-game battles, including tug-of-war, marbles, and red-green lights. The interesting thing is that it is even possible to explore the colorful main stairs and the dormitory.


The creators of this material, Ansimist, Concole and DepoSit, have also provided explanations about each of the available modes, which you can read in the following. Red Light, Green Light mode: You have to cross a field where any extra move can cost the player his life. Cookie Mode: A game in which you have to cut the cookie according to the specified shape. Tug of War Mode: As the name implies, players are divided into teams and begin to pull the rope quickly so as not to go out of range. Marbles mode: An old game. One of the players bets with marbles and at the same time tries to guess the opponent's bet. The Glass Tile Game Mode: Places a small bridge with ordinary and durable glass in front of the player. Crossing sturdy glass will lead you straight to victory. But ordinary glass is like a ticket to that world. Not a Squid Game mode: This stage is reached after the remaining players reach the end of the glass bridge. A knife is given to the players and they are placed in a one-on-one fight against each other. The last person to survive this fight will win. Would you like to follow in the footsteps of that character from the Squid Game series? So now go to the custom CS: GO Gabe Follower servers. You can also download the composite game mod here. The map of this material can accommodate a maximum of 64 players, and unfortunately we will not compete with 456 players.

If you do not know about this, we must say that Squid Game was able to overtake Bridgerton to become the most popular Netflix series of all time. It is unknown at this time what he will do after leaving the post. Huang Dong-hyuk, the creator of the series, has confirmed its return with the second season. Of course, with the unparalleled success of the first season of Squid Game, Netflix's making the second season of the series seemed obvious. The director and writer of the Squid Game series recently stated: "There is a lot of pressure, demand and interest to produce the second season. I almost feel like you made us have no choice but to produce a second season. There will definitely be a second season. I have the idea of ​​the next chapter in mind right now and I am currently in the planning stage. "But I think it's too early to talk about when it will be produced or broadcast or how it will happen."
