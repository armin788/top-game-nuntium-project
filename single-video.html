Completion of Rainbow Six Extraction game development process

  • videogame

In the latest game news, we learned that the production of Rainbow Six Extraction has finally ended. Ubisoft recently tweeted that the co-op shooting game has been released and is now being shipped for disc production and digital sales certification. Rainbow Six Extraction faced many development problems and was delayed many times since its introduction at E3 2019. Rainbow Six Extraction, inspired by the Outbreak mode of Rainbow Six Siege, shows the world being attacked by an alien parasite called Archaeans. Now the members of the .R.E.A.C.T team must enter the various alien activity centers, fight them, and keep the threats under control by taking potential risks. There are a total of 18 different operators in the Rainbow Six Extraction game to choose from, each with their own unique abilities; From Doc's healing abilities to Finka nanobots. Rainbow Six Extraction will be released on January 20, 1422 for the Xbox One, Xbox X Series, Xbox S Series, PlayStation 4, PlayStation 5, PC and Google Studios platforms. This game is sold with a price tag of $ 40. Stay tuned for gameplay videos and more details on Rainbow Six Extraction in the coming weeks.
