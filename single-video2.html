The first Spider-Man gameplay show in Marvel’s Avengers

  • videogame

Finally, Crystal Dynamics has released the first gameplay video of Spider-Man in Marvel's Avengers. A few hours ago, Crystal Dynamics Studio released a new video of Spider-Man character gameplay in Marvel’s Avengers game. This new show, published in collaboration with the IGN website, showcases parts of the character's capabilities and gadgets. According to this information, the control of Spider-Man in this game is very similar to the character of Black Panther, the main character of the previous expansion pack of the game. Unlike Marvel's Spider-Man in Insomniac Games, Spider-Man Marvel's Avengers has less variety in movement and simpler animations. Much of Spider-Man fights in this game take place on the ground in solitary confinement. According to the new show, flying in play environments is also designed to be limited, and sometimes Spider-Man strings are sometimes attached to the invisible roof in the sky instead of the surrounding buildings and heights during the flight. Like all other superheroes in the game, ‌ Spider-Man is equipped with three special Heroic Abilities. The first power, called the Web-Bomb, gives you the ability to land a large number of enemies at the same time using Spider-Man strings. The second feature is called The Spider-Drone, which activates a small spider drone to help you. When using this gadget, the spider drone will follow you during the fights and will shoot at the enemies at the same time. It is also possible to use this drone only in a specific area by specifying a specific area of ​​the battlefield. The last special power of Spider-Man, the most powerful of which is called Wrecking Ball. By activating this special power, which bears many similarities to Thor's biofrost force, you can damage a large number of enemies by hitting a huge ball of Spider-Man strings to the ground. This new update, which is exclusive to PlayStation console players, will be available for download next week, Tuesday, November 30th. You can watch the first gameplay of Spider-Man character below.
